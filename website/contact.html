Common Questions

How quickly can I expect a response?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 2-3 business days. For urgent matters, please call the school office at 408-535-6310.

Can I attend a board meeting?

Yes! Board meetings are open to observers. Please contact us in advance to receive the Zoom meeting details.

How do I apply for a scholarship?

Scholarship information and applications are distributed through Pioneer High School counselors in the spring. Contact your counselor for details.

Can I donate items instead of money?

Yes! We accept in-kind donations. Please contact us to discuss your donation and coordinate delivery or pickup.

How do I update my contact information or donation preferences?

Simply send us an email with your updated information, and we'll make the changes promptly.
