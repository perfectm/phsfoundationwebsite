Physics Dynamics Cart & Track System

Fund a Vernier Dynamics Cart and Track System with Go Direct Sensor Carts for our physics classroom. This hands-on equipment features wireless motorized sensor carts that allow students to investigate motion, forces, Newton's laws, momentum, and collisions with real-time data collection and analysis.
This equipment is used by the entire freshman class.

Legacy & Planned Giving

Create a lasting impact for future generations of Pioneer students

Legacy gifts ensure that Pioneer High School students benefit from exceptional educational opportunities for generations to come. Your planned gift can take many forms and may provide significant tax benefits for you and your family.

Bequests

Include the foundation in your will or living trust. This simple, flexible option allows you to maintain control of your assets during your lifetime.

Beneficiary Designations

Name the foundation as a beneficiary of your retirement account, life insurance policy, or other financial accounts.

Memorial & Tribute Gifts

Honor a loved one, celebrate a special occasion, or create a named fund that reflects your values and interests.

Charitable Trusts

Establish a charitable remainder or lead trust that provides income to you or your family while supporting the foundation.

Endowment Gifts

Create a permanent source of funding that generates ongoing support for specific programs or scholarships.

Stock & Securities

Donate appreciated securities and potentially avoid capital gains taxes while receiving a charitable deduction.

Sample Bequest Language

"I give [specific dollar amount, percentage of estate, or description of property] to Pioneer High School Education Foundation, a California non-profit corporation, Tax ID 20-5727442, located in San Jose, California, for its general purposes [or for the specific purpose of ___________]."
